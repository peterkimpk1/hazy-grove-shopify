Our Story

A working hemp farm with a commitment to quality

Rooted in China Grove, NC

Hazy Grove Hemp Farm sits on Daugherty Road in China Grove, North Carolina. Bobby started growing hemp here because he believed people deserve better — better products, better transparency, and a real connection to where their hemp comes from.

Every product starts in our fields. We grow our own hemp, then work with trusted partners to craft it into the gummies and tinctures you know and love. Every batch is independently lab tested, every ingredient is listed, and every question gets an honest answer. That's the Hazy Grove way.

What We Stand For

The principles that guide everything we do

Transparency

We grow our own hemp and test every batch through independent labs so you know exactly what you're getting. Full ingredient lists, cannabinoid breakdowns, and third-party lab results — no fine print, no surprises.

Quality First

From our fields to your hands, every step meets our standards for purity and potency. We never cut corners — every batch is independently tested by ISO-certified labs.

Community

We're proud to be part of China Grove and the wider North Carolina community. Building a local business and connecting people with quality hemp products is at the heart of what we do.
