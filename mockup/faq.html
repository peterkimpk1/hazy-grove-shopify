Products

What types of products does Hazy Grove Farms offer?
We offer three main product lines: CBD Daily Gummies (available in Watermelon, Blood Orange, and Blue Raspberry), THC Recreational Gummies (available in Indica, Sativa, and Hybrid blends), Sleep Gummies (with CBD + THC + CBN), and Tinctures (Full Spectrum and THC-Free options). All products are crafted with premium hemp and independently lab tested.
What's the difference between CBD Daily and THC Recreational gummies?
CBD Daily Gummies are designed for everyday wellness with 50mg CBD and a small amount of THC (2mg) per gummy. They're great for general balance, stress, and recovery. THC Recreational Gummies contain 15mg THC + 10mg CBD + 2mg CBN per gummy and are formulated for a noticeable recreational experience. They come in Indica (relax), Sativa (energize), and Hybrid (balanced) blends.
Do you offer THC-free products?
Yes! We offer THC-Free CBD Daily Gummies (50mg CBD, 0mg THC) in all three flavors, as well as a THC-Free Tincture and THC-Free Sleep Tincture. These are ideal for anyone who wants the benefits of CBD without any THC, including those subject to drug testing.
What is "Extra Strength" and how is it different?
Our Extra Strength line doubles the cannabinoid content. CBD Daily Extra Strength contains 100mg CBD + 5mg THC per gummy (vs. 50mg CBD + 2mg THC standard). Sleep Extra Strength contains 100mg CBD + 5mg THC + 5mg CBN per gummy. Extra Strength products are recommended for experienced users or those who need a higher dose.

Dosage & Usage

How many gummies should I take?
We recommend starting with one gummy per serving. If you're new to CBD or THC products, consider starting with half a gummy and working your way up. Everyone responds differently, so it's best to find what works for you. For our THC Recreational gummies, wait at least 60–90 minutes before considering a second dose.
How long does it take to feel the effects?
Gummies are ingested orally, so effects typically onset within 30–60 minutes, depending on your metabolism, body weight, and whether you've eaten recently. Effects generally last 4–6 hours. Tinctures taken sublingually (under the tongue) may onset faster, within 15–30 minutes.
When should I take Sleep gummies?
Take one Sleep gummy approximately 30–60 minutes before bedtime. The combination of CBD, THC, and CBN (cannabinol) is formulated to promote relaxation and support a restful night's sleep. Avoid operating machinery after taking Sleep gummies.
How should I store my products?
Store all products in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight. Keep jars sealed between uses to preserve freshness. Gummies do not require refrigeration but may benefit from it in warmer climates. All products have a shelf life of approximately 12 months from manufacturing date.

Legal & Safety

Are your products legal?
Yes. All Hazy Grove Farms products are derived from hemp and comply with the 2018 Farm Bill, which legalized hemp and hemp-derived products containing less than 0.3% THC by dry weight at the federal level. However, some states have enacted additional restrictions — please check your local laws before purchasing.
Will your products show up on a drug test?
Products containing THC (including our Standard and Extra Strength CBD Daily, THC Recreational, and Sleep gummies) may cause a positive result on a drug test. If you are subject to drug testing, we recommend our THC-Free product line, which contains 0mg THC and is made with broad-spectrum extract.
Do you provide lab results?
Absolutely. Every batch is tested by an independent, ISO-certified third-party laboratory. Certificates of Analysis (COAs) verify potency, purity, and safety — including tests for heavy metals, pesticides, microbials, residual solvents, and mycotoxins. COA links are available on each product page.
How old do I have to be to purchase?
You must be at least 21 years old to purchase THC-containing products from Hazy Grove Farms. An age verification gate is required before accessing the website. CBD-only (THC-Free) products require you to be at least 18 years old.
